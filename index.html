How to Play Color Crafter

Try to craft the top color by combining the bottom three colors. You may select a color more than once to increase its prominence in the mix. Once you have selected six colors, click the button that appears on the left to combine your selection. Your combination will be given a percentage based on how closely it matches the target color. You have five attempts to craft the target color. Good Luck!
